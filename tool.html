How Plankworth works

1

Snap & valuate

Upload a tree photo. Our AI identifies the species, estimates board feet from the trunk diameter, and gives you a defensible value range.

2

Mills bid

If the wood is salvage-grade, we route your lead to vetted, insured portable sawmill operators in a 50-mile radius. They contact you within 48 hours with cash offers.

3

Get paid · free haul

Pick the offer you like. The mill removes the tree at no charge and pays you for the wood. We take a 15% commission from the mill — never from you.
